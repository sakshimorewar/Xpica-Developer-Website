Geo Tagging Mobile App

Smart Tree Mapping for Sustainable Urban Planning
Our team has developed a robust cross-platform mobile application for geo-tagging trees with precision.
The app automatically captures GPS coordinates (latitude and longitude) while tagging, enabling
accurate tracking of tree plantations, including residential and forest area counts.
This solution is currently deployed by the Chhatrapati Sambhajinagar (Aurangabad)
Municipal Corporation (AMC), supporting efficient plantation audits and green cover monitoring
through smart automation.
